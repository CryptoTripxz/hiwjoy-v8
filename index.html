⚡ What is a Calorie?
A calorie is a unit of energy. Your body uses calories to breathe, think, move, and repair. 1 kcal = energy to raise 1kg of water by 1°C. Food gives you calories. Movement spends them.
2,000avg daily need (women)
2,500avg daily need (men)
3,500kcal = 1 lb of fat
1
Find your TDEE 🎯

Skin Science 101 ✨
Understand your skin to actually fix it · +350 XP
▾
🔬 Your Skin is an Organ
Skin is the body's largest organ. Most "miracle" products only reach the epidermis. True skin health comes from hydration, sleep, nutrition and SPF.
28 daysskin cell turnover
SPF 30+prevents 97% of UVB rays
70%of ageing is sun damage
1
Identify your skin type 🔍
Wash face, wait 30 mins, no product. Oily all over = oily. Flaky + tight = dry.
✓
2
The 3-step non-negotiables 🌿
Cleanse · Moisturise · SPF every morning. Everything else is optional.
⏱ Start tonight with these 3
✓
3
Active ingredients decoded 🧪
Retinol (PM) · Vitamin C (AM) · Niacinamide (any time) · Hyaluronic acid (damp skin).
✓
4
Diet-skin connection 🥗
High glycaemic foods trigger acne. Omega-3s reduce inflammation. 8 glasses of water improves skin elasticity in 4 weeks.
✓
5
Build your AM/PM routine 🌅
AM: Cleanse → Vitamin C → Moisturiser → SPF. PM: Double cleanse → Retinol → Moisturiser.
